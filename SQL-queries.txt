Total number of banks in the database per Bank corp.:
SELECT Bank, COUNT(bank) AS NumOfBanks
FROM external_account
GROUP BY bank
ORDER BY numOfBanks DESC;

Total number of external accounts per subscriber:
SELECT s.SubscriberID, s.Username, s.FName,s.LName,COUNT(e.bank) AS TotalOfExternalAccount
FROM subscriber_account AS S, external_account AS E
WHERE s.subscriberid = e.subscriberid
GROUP BY s.subscriberid
Order by TotalOfExternalAccount DESC;
